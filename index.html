CAT POEM NUMBER 3

They say we are brothers though we look nothing alike
They say we are family though we differ so much
I am tabby, you are brown
I am long, you are short
I am thin, you are stout
I am lively, you are shy
I am a kitten, you are a hamster
But kin is kin
So let me run on your wheel

I lick your nose
I lick your nose again
I drag my claws down your eyelids
Oh, you're up? Feed me

This table is cold. That hand is cold
The needle is cold. The stethoscope is cold
The doctor is cold. The nurse is cold
The room is cold
The only thing that's warm
Is the puddle of urine
I made on the vet's notes
